# Lab 02: Phishing Email Investigation

## Lab Status

In Progress

## Objective

The objective of this lab is to investigate a simulated phishing email by analyzing its sender information, email headers, authentication results, links, social-engineering techniques, and indicators of compromise.

## Tools Used

- Raw `.eml` email file
- Windows Notepad
- Email header analysis
- GitHub
- MITRE ATT&CK

## Investigation Scenario

A user reported an urgent email claiming that unusual activity was detected on their Microsoft account.

The message instructed the user to verify their identity within 30 minutes by clicking a link.

The goal of this investigation is to determine whether the email is legitimate, suspicious, or malicious.

> This lab uses safe simulated information. No real malicious infrastructure is used.

## Investigation Steps

1. Review the sender and display name.
2. Compare the sender and reply-to addresses.
3. Examine the sending IP address.
4. Review SPF, DKIM, and DMARC results.
5. Examine the email for urgent or threatening language.
6. Compare the visible link with its actual destination.
7. Document suspicious indicators and IOCs.
8. Assign an analyst verdict.
9. Recommend containment and response actions.
